About Skyvan

Decades of Expertise Across the Americas

Skyvan Logistics is a Miami-based aviation services company with over 30 years of hands-on experience in global aviation operations. What began as deep regional expertise in Latin American aviation operations has grown into a full-spectrum logistics, sourcing, and support company serving both fixed-wing and rotary-wing clients.

We operate where others cannot. Our network spans manufacturers, operators, government entities, and supply chains across Latin America and the United States — built over decades of trust, not transactions.
